Section 3 · Regulatory Compliance — Formulae & Effort Data

CRAR · SLR · CRR · ECL — Formulae & Automation Impact

Sources: S-08 (RBI Master Circular 2024), S-12 (NABARD Diagnostic Study 2024). Compliance engine is the highest-ROI internal product: 780+ person-hours → 90 hours per cycle; 63-day cycle → 24 days.

CRAR · S-08
CRAR = (T1 + T2) ÷ RWA × 100
Minimum: 9% for cooperative banks (RBI Master Circular 2024)
T1 = Tier 1: paid-up capital + reserves
T2 = Tier 2: revaluation reserves + subordinated debt
RWA = Risk-Weighted Assets: each asset × risk weight
SLR · S-08
SLR = SLR Securities ÷ NDTL × 100
Minimum: 18.00% of NDTL, computed fortnightly (RBI 2024)
SLR Securities = G-Secs, NABARD bonds, state govt. securities
NDTL = Net Demand & Time Liabilities (savings + FD + current, net interbank)
ECL (IFRS 9) · S-08
ECL = PD × LGD × EAD
Expected Credit Loss — NPA EWS provisioning basis (IFRS 9)
PD = Probability of Default (AI/ML score)
LGD = Loss Given Default (collateral coverage)
EAD = Exposure at Default (outstanding balance)

RBI Risk Weights for CRAR (S-08)

Asset CategoryRisk WeightCapital Impact
Govt Securities (G-Secs)0%No capital required
AAA-Rated Corporate Bonds20%Low capital charge

Critical Insight: A ₹1 crore NPA requires ₹1.5 crore RWA coverage at 9% CRAR = ₹13.5 lakh minimum capital. A performing KCC loan needs only ₹6.75 lakh. EWS prevents NPA formation — directly preserving CRAR headroom.

Section 5 · NPA Early Warning System — Signal Logic & Recovery Data

NPA EWS — Signal Hierarchy, Recovery Rates & ECL Logic

Sources: S-06 (NABARD Microfinance Status 2022–23), S-08 (RBI Master Circular 2024), S-14 (NABARD Maharashtra AI Pilot 2024). EWS flags accounts 30+ days before the 90-DPD NPA threshold — raising recovery from 42% to 78%.

🍺 Green — Watch (0 DPD)

All repayments on schedule. NDVI within seasonal average. NACH clearing normally. No insurance claim in crop zone. Action: nil. Recovery probability: >95%.

🍸 Amber — Alert (5–30 DPD)

Repayment delayed 5–30 DPD. Account utilisation drop >20%. NACH bounced once. NDVI drop >15%. Action: RM call within 14 days, restructure offer. Recovery: 78% (S-06)

🍴 Orange — Risk (30–60 DPD)

DPD 30–60. IMD drought flag active. NACH bounce ×2 consecutive months. Cluster signal: 5+ PACS accounts defaulting. Action: Field officer visit + OTS offer. Recovery: 60–70%.

🔴 Red — NPA (DPD ≥90)

RBI 90-day DPD classification triggers. CBS auto-flags as NPA. Legal notice initiated. Provisioning: Sub-Standard 15%, Doubtful Yr1 25%, Yr2+ 40%, Loss 100%. Recovery without early action: 42% (S-06).
